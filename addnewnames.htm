Suppose a new community is built in your market.  An even more likely scenario is a county being added to your ADI. Town Chooser Version 2.0 allows you to add new towns to the database with a minimal of effort.

    The first thing you need to do is click the Add button.  This will clear the Town Name textbox.

Your next step will be to type in the name of the new town you want to add to the database (make sure you spell it right or you'll be finding and then editing it later).

    Now all you have to do is click the Update button and the town name is added to the database.
