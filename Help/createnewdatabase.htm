The database that is supplied with Town Chooser Version 2.0 contains over 350 towns and communities that are in the Jonesboro, Arkansas market covered by KAIT, Channel Eight.  Unfortunately, if you're not working for KAIT or a station in Jonesboro market, this database is not much use to you.

There are a couple of ways that you can get a database that contains towns and communities in your market.

  1. Contact us and for a one time fee of only $50.00, we will create a database of 250+ cities, towns, and communities in your market for your exclusive use.
  2. Delete each town name from the current database and add the town names from your market yourself.
  3. Use Microsoft® Access 97 to delete the records from the current database and then add the town names from your market either using Microsoft® Access 97 or using Town Chooser Version 2.0.

Any of these three methods will work.
